// WHAT IT DOES

not just another
doc generator

🗺️
system-level understanding
Crawls your entire repo, builds a static import graph, groups files by actual relationships — not arbitrary size.
💥
blast radius mapping
Every service doc leads with risk level, dependents, and exactly what breaks if you touch it.
🔁
checkpoint-safe
Saves progress after every chunk. Crash at chunk 38/47? Resume from exactly where you left off.
🤖
AI-agnostic
Works with Claude, GPT, or any provider. Switch with one config line. Your key, your machine, zero backend cost.
👁️
watch mode
Incremental re-analysis on file change. Context stays fresh as your codebase evolves.
🌍
team-level context
One engineer runs it, the whole team benefits. Persistent, shareable, compounds over time.
